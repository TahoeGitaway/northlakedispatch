Hot Tub Billing

The app counts & classifies every hot-tub service from Breezeway so you can bill owners by hand in Streamline. This is a read-only worksheet — it never writes to Breezeway and never bills. Classification reads the task title/description text (typos tolerated); task tags are shown for context only and never affect billing. Confirm every flagged row before billing.
